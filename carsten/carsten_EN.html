Game Rules

Carsten's Cube is a strategic game for two players. The players take turns selecting shapes and placing them on the playing field. The objective is to form a row, column, or diagonal of four shapes that are either all the same or all different. The first player to form a complete row, column, or diagonal with the specified criteria wins!
